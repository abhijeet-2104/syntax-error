Benefits of Yoga

Yoga is a mind and body practice that originated in ancient India. It combines physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation to promote overall health and well-being. The word "yoga" comes from the Sanskrit word "yuj," which means to yoke or unite, symbolizing the union of body and mind.
